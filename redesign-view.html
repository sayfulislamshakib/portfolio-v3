About
I am leading a project to improve and optimize an existing web application dashboard designed for banking experiences. The initial design was created by a UX designer, and my role is to refine and enhance the overall user experience.
Why Redesign?
The current design presents opportunities for improvement in terms of visual aesthetics, information presentation, accessibility, and user-centricity. Below are key areas where enhancements can be made to elevate the overall user experience:
  • Visual Aesthetics: The current design does not meet the desired level of visual perfection. Aesthetic improvements should be considered to create a more visually appealing and engaging user interface.
  • Information Presentation: Information is currently not optimally displayed within the design. Enhancements are needed to ensure that information is presented in a clear, organized, and user-friendly manner, making it easier for users to digest and interact with the content.
  • Accessibility: The existing design falls short in terms of accessibility. It's imperative to make accessibility a top priority, adhering to best practices and standards to ensure that all users, including those with disabilities, can seamlessly access and navigate the platform.
  • User-Centric Approach: The current design lacks consistency in information presentation. A user-centric approach should be adopted to ensure that all content and messaging are aligned with user expectations, needs, and preferences.
Problem
From a user's perspective, we can observe that users cannot obtain the required information accurately. The design lacks proper color balance, resulting in a subpar user experience and accessibility issues from a visual standpoint.
Let's start with color
The current design features an improper and unbalanced use of color palettes, resulting in visual chaos. In addition, it has created a confusing and cluttered visual experience for users, which may lead to a lack of engagement and difficulty navigating the platform. Therefore, revising the colour palettes and implementing a balanced colour scheme will improve the overall design and user experience.
Research
Based on discussions with the client and primary users of the product, I have identified key points that require attention. To enhance usability for the primary users, I have undertaken a redesign effort that seeks to simplify the product. I have developed a new visual identity and comprehensive design system for all touchpoints to achieve this. The goal is to create a consistent, intuitive product experience across all channels, resulting in greater user satisfaction and engagement.
