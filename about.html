About me
I was born in Bangladesh, where my experiences with diverse environments and people taught me the importance of adaptability, perspective, and understanding. These skills have been essential in my career as a designer.
As a child, I loved drawing and observing the best design in the world. I also loved helping others. This passion drives my work today: I want to help people and businesses succeed through design.
I am particularly interested in designing solutions to complex problems. I am motivated by the challenge of simplifying complexity through intentionality and empowering people.
I want to work for companies that appreciate the craft and have a purpose I can resonate with. Ultimately, I want to design products that help people create value for themselves and others as quickly and efficiently as possible.
