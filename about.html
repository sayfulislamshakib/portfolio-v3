About me
Born and raised in Bangladesh , as a child, I loved working behind the scenes in school plays, whether as the light or music technician, or designing props; I enjoyed doing things that helped the actors on stage shine. This same passion extends to design for me in the present day: I want to help other people and businesses shine and do better work.
My passion for design is about serving and helping people. I’m driven by ambiguity and love to simplify through intentionality, empowering others through thoughtful designs with a high level of craft.
TL;DR. I want to build products that help people autonomously and efficiently create value for themselves and others.
