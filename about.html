About me
I was born in Bangladesh, where I learned the importance of being adaptable, gaining different perspectives, and understanding various cultures. These skills have proven crucial in my career as a designer.
As a child, I had a passion for drawing and admiring excellent designs. I also had a strong desire to assist others. This passion continues to drive my work today. I aim to assist people and businesses in achieving success through design.
I am particularly interested in creating solutions for complicated issues. I am motivated by the challenge of simplifying complex problems through careful planning and empowering individuals.
I'm looking to work for companies that value the art of design and have a mission I can connect with. Ultimately, my goal is to design products that help people quickly and effectively generate value for themselves and others.
